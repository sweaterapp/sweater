Sweater

Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 24, 2026

Sweater is a social fitness accountability app. This Privacy Policy explains what information Sweater collects, how it is used, and what choices you have.

Information We Collect

  • Account information such as your email address, display name, and handle.
  • Profile information you choose to add, such as a profile photo, city, or favorite gym.
  • App activity such as partner connections, challenge invitations, challenge participation, proof uploads, and workout notes.
  • Basic device and app usage information needed to keep the app working reliably.

How We Use Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To show your profile, challenges, partners, history, and proof uploads inside the app.
  • To deliver core app features such as authentication, reminders, and challenge updates.
  • To maintain app security, investigate bugs, and improve the product.

Photos and Proof Uploads

If you upload proof photos or a profile photo, those files are stored so they can appear in the app for you and, where relevant, for the other participants in your challenges.

How Information Is Shared

Sweater does not sell your personal information. Information is shared only as needed to operate the app, including with service providers that support authentication, storage, and app infrastructure.

Data Retention

Sweater keeps account and challenge data for as long as reasonably necessary to operate the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

Your Choices

  • You can update parts of your profile inside the app.
  • You can sign out at any time.
  • You can request account or data support by contacting the email below.

Children’s Privacy

Sweater is not intended for children under 13, and it should not be used by anyone who is not legally allowed to use the service in their region.

Changes to This Policy

Sweater may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If that happens, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

Contact

For privacy questions or support requests, contact sweater.appins@gmail.com.
