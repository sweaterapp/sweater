Support

If you need help with your account, challenge activity, proof uploads, or anything else in Sweater, reach out and we’ll take a look.

Email Support

Best things to include

  • Your account email.
  • A short description of the issue.
  • Screenshots if something looks wrong.
  • The approximate time the problem happened.

Account and data requests

If you need help with account access, password resets, or a data/privacy request, email the same support inbox and mention the request clearly in the subject line.
