How it works

Choose your crew. Set the goal. Show up.

Sweater is built around recurring weekly accountability instead of one-off check-ins, so your progress keeps rolling with the people who will actually notice if you disappear.

Built for

Friends who need a little pressure to stay consistent.

Less wellness fluff, more follow-through.

What you can do in Sweater

Partner up

Add friends by handle, manage requests, and build a small accountability circle that actually matters.

Start challenges

Create recurring pacts, choose a start date, optionally add an end date, and bring multiple partners into one challenge.

Keep receipts

Every challenge lives with proof history, score summaries, and a clear record of who stayed on track.
